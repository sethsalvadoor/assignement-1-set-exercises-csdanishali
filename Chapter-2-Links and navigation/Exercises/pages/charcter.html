Characters

You’re the main character. Sorry. But the game does revolve around customizable avatars that can take on various job classes, each with unique abilities and specializations:

  • Swordsman/Knight/Lord Knight – Your standard tank with anger issues
  • Acolyte/Priest/High Priest – Healers who beg for party invites and die first anyway
  • Thief/Assassin/Assassin Cross – Sneaky types who stab things and look cool doing it
  • Mage/Wizard/High Wizard – Glass cannons with a superiority complex
  • Archer/Hunter/Sniper – For people who like damage from a safe emotional distance
  • Merchant/Blacksmith/Whitesmith – Capitalists with hammers

NPCs include:

  • Prontera Guard – Always useless
  • King Tristan III – Still not doing anything
  • Kafra Ladies – Who truly run the world (and your storage)
  • Poring – A gelatinous mascot with a thousand variations and inexplicable power
