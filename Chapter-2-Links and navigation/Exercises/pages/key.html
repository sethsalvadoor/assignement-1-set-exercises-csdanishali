Plot & Key Information

Plot? Ha. It’s an MMORPG. There is lore, sure, but let’s be honest—most players skim the dialogue and chase XP. That said, the game loosely follows the eternal conflict between gods, humans, and demons after the destruction of the world tree, Yggdrasil. From this mythological fallout arises a fractured world filled with adventure, mystery, and a suspicious number of talking cats.

  • Classic 2.5D sprite-over-3D style graphics
  • The original six job classes with tiered advancements
  • Real-time PvP and War of Emperium
  • Pet, mount, and equipment systems
  • A thriving marketplace and economy
  • Daily quests, dungeons, and seasonal events
